Use Case 1: Move chess piece
Scope: Chess application
Primary Actor: Player
Preconditions: Player has started a game
Postconditions: Player has moved a piece
Stakeholders and Interests:
- Player: Player wants to move pieces until opponent's king is in check
Main Success Scenario:
1. Player choose piece to move.
2. Player tries to move piece from piece's current standing position to player's chosen end position.
3. Move is being validated.
4. Attempted move is approved
5. Piece is moved to chosen end position.
Extensions:
4a. Attempted move is declined
-4a1. Piece is moved back to previous position before player's attempted move
