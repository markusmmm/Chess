Use Case 1: Moving chess piece
Scope: Chess application
Level: User goal
Primary Actor: Player
Stakeholders and Interests:
- Player: Player wants to move pieces until opponent's king is in check
Success Guarantee: Player does legal move.
Basic flow:
1. Player choose piece to move.
2. Player tries to move piece from pieces current standing position to player's chosen end position.
3. Move is being validated.
4. Attempted move is approved
4. Piece is moved to chosen end position.
Alternative flow:
1. Procedure preceeds as in basic flow until step 3
2. Move is declined
3. Piece stays on current position
